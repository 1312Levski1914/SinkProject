HOW Does it Work?


We send our staff to visit you house and install cold/hot potable water mechanical valve which is mounted to the wall under the sink using supplied bushings and screws, with the water flow directions arrow pointing up. The valve's inlet ports connect to the water supply hoses provided with the unit. The outlet ports connect to the hot/cold hoses from the faucet.
