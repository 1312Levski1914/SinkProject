About us

Alexandrs Petrovs

Alexandrs is the founder of the MotoAqua brand. He has always been an environmentalist at heart so it was no surprise that this idea clicked for him. He got the idea when travelling in Mexico, where he saw a lot of these pedals in all sorts of places, and knew then that he needed to bring it to Denmark.

Elize Kupce

Elize is the cofounder of Motoaqua. She lives a minimalistic and environmentally friendly lifestyle. So it did not take her a long time before she decided to join Alexandrs on this adventure.

Filip Krajco

After being a devoted costumer of Motoaqua since it first came on the market, the company decided to hire Filip. He has been an important part of the team since 2020.
